History of Gyoza

Gyoza, a beloved food in Japan, believed to be introduced from China in the 19th Century.

Its popularity among Japanese sky rocketed after World War II, while returning solidiers was craving for the iconic China street food, JiaoJi.

Today, gyoza is a staple part of Japanese cuisine, readily found in ramen shops, Chinese restaurants, specialty stores, and even convenience stores.
